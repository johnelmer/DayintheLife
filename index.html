Through the eyes of an MSBA Student

How did the Fall Quarter Go?

It has been a marathon! We have just started the program but we've come a long way and we've already learned a lot. But what did we really do in the Fall Quarter? Where did we go? How does it feel like to be an MSBA student? This personal blog aims to show the things I did in the last quarter and aims to detail my experience as a UC Davis MSBA Student.

Where do I usually go?

With classes happening only on Friday and Saturday, I spend most of the remaining time to visit scenic places in San Francisco, spend time with classmates as well as go on meetups within the city. While it is true that joining the program can be stressful, one should still look for ways to destress and enjoy the fact that we're in San Francisco.

How do I spend my 24 hours?

More than half of my daily routine is centered at home or in school.

Specific Activity on a Specific Day

Fridays are for School. Sundays and Mondays are for hikes.

Background

Throught the Eyes of an MSBA Student was a visualziation created by John Elmer Loretizo. The data for this visualization was taken from Life Cycle with the design inspiration coming from Rythm of Food. This visualization is centered on describing the creator's quarter as an MSBA student at University of California, Davis. It gives a perspective as to how his typical day goes and his week is like.
